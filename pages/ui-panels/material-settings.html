Overview

The Material Settings panel configures how Blender materials are exported for Meta Horizon Studio. BlendMHS materials are designed for 1:1 mapping to MHS renderer_module shaders.

Material Properties

Base Properties

  • Base Color: Fallback color when no BC texture is assigned
  • Metallic: 0.0 (dielectric) to 1.0 (metal)
  • Roughness: 0.0 (smooth) to 1.0 (rough)

Advanced Properties

  • Alpha Mode: Opaque, Blend, or Mask
  • Alpha Cutoff: Threshold for masked transparency
  • Double Sided: Render both sides of faces
  • Emission Strength: Intensity multiplier for emissive materials

MHS Shader Parity

Materials created in BlendMHS are designed to render identically in MHS:

  • Same texture inputs and naming conventions
  • Matching PBR parameters and ranges
  • Consistent normal map handling
  • Accurate emission and transparency
