Overview

The Project Management panel is the starting point for setting up your BlendMHS project. It defines where your exported USD files, textures, and materials will be saved, and establishes the project structure that Meta Horizon Studio expects.

Panel Location

The Project Management panel is located in the 3D Viewport sidebar under the BlendMHS tab.
